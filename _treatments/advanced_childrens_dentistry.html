Managing children on the dental chair is a specialized task which involves scientifically proven techniques. This is done through special behaviour management techniques which involve motivating the child and eliminating unreasonable fear of the dentist's chair.

Abnormal oral habits in children are-

Thumb sucking, Tongue thrusting, Mouth breathing, Bruxism, Nail biting, lip biting etc. No treatment needed if habit stopped by 6-7 years of age or before erupting permanent dentition. But if persist these are responsible to produce malocclusion in permanent dentition. IHaris Family Dentistry provide different habit breaking appliances to eliminate these abnormal oral habits and prevent further malocclusion.
